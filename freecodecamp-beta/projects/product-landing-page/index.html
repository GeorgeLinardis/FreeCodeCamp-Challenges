What is Free Code Camp?

  • It is an open source community that helps you learn to code.
  • You can work through their self-paced coding challenges, build projects, and earn certificates.
    They also connect you with people in your city so you can code together.
  • Thousands of people have gotten software developer jobs after joining the open source community.
  • IT IS FREE. Every aspect of the program - their curriculum, nonprofit projects, and verified certificates - is 100% free.
